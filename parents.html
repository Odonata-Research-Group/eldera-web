The journey

From Sign-Up to First Session —
Here’s Exactly What Happens.

1

Create your family’s profile

Tell us about your child — their name, age, interests, and anything you’d want a mentor to know. Takes about 10 minutes.

2

We find your match

Our team matches your child with a vetted mentor based on personality, interests, availability, and life experience. You’ll see your mentor’s full profile before committing.

3

You approve, then meet

For children under 13, you approve every session. The first session, you’re present. After that, Eldera’s AI Chaperone monitors every call — and you receive a summary.

4

Weekly sessions begin

One hour, once a week, over Eldera’s secure platform. Your child and their mentor build a real relationship at whatever pace feels right.

5

You stay in the loop

Session summaries, milestone moments, and check-ins keep you informed without intruding on your child’s growing independence.

What to expect as a parent

The first few sessions are warm-up. The magic tends to happen around weeks 4–6. Commitment makes it work.
