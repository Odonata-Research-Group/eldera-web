The journey

From Sign-Up to First Session —
Here’s Exactly What Happens.

1

Create your family’s profile

Tell us about your child — their name, age, interests, and anything you’d want a mentor to know. Takes about 10 minutes.

2

We find your match

Our team matches your child with a vetted mentor based on personality, interests, availability, and life experience. You’ll see their full profile before anything is confirmed.

3

You approve, then meet

For children under 13, you approve every session. You’re present for the first one. After that, Eldera’s AI Chaperone monitors every call and sends you a summary.

4

Weekly sessions begin

One hour, once a week, over Eldera’s secure platform. Your child and their mentor build a real relationship at whatever pace feels right.

5

You stay in the loop

Session summaries, milestone moments, and check-ins keep you informed without intruding on your child’s growing independence.

Safety first

How We Keep Your Child Safe.

Safety isn’t a feature — it’s the foundation everything else is built on.

Background check — every mentor

No exceptions, no shortcuts. Every mentor is fully screened before being matched with any child.

AI Chaperone on every call

Real-time monitoring across 12 risk categories. A silent safeguard — never an active participant.

Parents always in control

You approve sessions and receive summaries after every call. Children under 13 have fully parent-managed accounts.

Human review on every flag

Every safety escalation is reviewed by a real Eldera team member. No automated-only responses, ever.

Mentor training & orientation

Every mentor completes training on expectations, safe communication, and child development before their first session.

Eldera operates under an active MOU with the California Commission on Aging and meets
WHO Social Prescription standards for safe intergenerational programming.

What to expect as a parent

A recurring weekly slot

Sessions happen at the same time each week — easy to build into your family’s routine.

Session summaries in your inbox

After every session, you get a brief summary of what was discussed — so you’re always informed.

We ask you to stay engaged

The relationship works best when parents are involved. Occasionally approve a session or respond to a note — usually within 24 hours.

Real relationships take time

The first few sessions are warm-up. The magic tends to happen around weeks 4–6. Commitment makes it work.
