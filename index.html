Why Eldera

Not a Tutoring App. Not a Volunteer Program.
Something New.

Eldera is social infrastructure built to solve loneliness at both ends of the generational divide, at scale.

AI Chaperone

Proprietary safety technology monitors every session in real time — protecting mentor and mentee without intruding on natural conversation.

Wisdom Matching

We match on personality, interests, life experience, and availability — not just age and location. Real bonds form from real alignment.

Social Health Tracker

A first-of-its-kind tool that visualizes your social health over time — sessions, emotional impact, and contribution streaks.

Community Beyond 1:1

Mentor Councils, community events, and async Q&A threads mean you’re always part of something larger than one relationship.

Institutional Trust

Active MOU with the State of California. WHO Social Prescription qualification. Columbia Mailman School research partnership.

Pay It Forward Model

Eldera runs on generosity. Members who can contribute fund sessions for families who can’t. No family is ever turned away.
