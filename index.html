Simple by design

Start Simple. Make an Impact.

Four steps from sign-up to your first session. We walk alongside you the whole way.

01

Create Your Profile

Tell us who you are in about 15 minutes: your interests, life experience, and what you’d love to pass on to the next generation.

02

Choose How You Show Up

Mentor weekly one-on-one, join community events, or answer questions on your own schedule. Your time, your terms.

03

Meet Your Match

We pair you on personality, interests, and life experience, not just availability. Your first session is guided and supported.

04

See Your Impact

Your Social Health Tracker shows sessions, streaks, and the lives you’re touching, week by week and month by month.

01

Apply for Your Child

Tell us about your child: their age, interests, and what you’re hoping they’ll get from the experience. Takes about 10 minutes.

02

We Find the Right Fit

Our team reviews your child’s profile and selects a vetted, background-checked mentor whose life experience and personality genuinely align with theirs.

03

The First Session

You’re introduced to your child’s mentor before they meet. The first session is structured and warm. We make sure everyone feels at ease from the start.

04

Watch Them Grow

Follow along with your family dashboard. Every session is AI-chaperoned for safety, and you’ll see the relationship deepen week by week.

Why Eldera

Not a Tutoring App. Not a Volunteer Program.
Something New.

Eldera is social infrastructure built to solve loneliness at both ends of the generational divide, at scale.

AI Chaperone

Proprietary safety technology monitors every session in real time, protecting both sides of the conversation without ever intruding on it.

Wisdom Matching

We match on personality, interests, life experience, and availability. Not just age and location. Real bonds form from real alignment.

Social Health Tracker

A first-of-its-kind tool that visualizes your social health over time: sessions, emotional impact, and contribution streaks.

Community Beyond 1:1

Mentor Councils, community events, and async Q&A threads mean you’re always part of something larger than one relationship.

Institutional Trust

Active MOU with the State of California. WHO Social Prescription qualification. Columbia Mailman School research partnership.

Pay It Forward Model

Eldera runs on generosity. Members who can contribute fund sessions for families who can’t. No family is ever turned away.
