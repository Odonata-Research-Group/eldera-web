The process

From Sign-Up to First Session:
Here’s Exactly What Happens.

1

Create your Wisdom Profile

Tell us who you are: your background, interests, life experiences, and what you’d like to share with a young person. Takes about 15 minutes.

2

Background check & orientation

Every mentor completes a secure background check and a short orientation covering expectations, safety, and communication norms. Typically takes 5–7 days.

3

Get matched

We match you with a young person whose interests and needs align with your experience. You see their profile before anything is confirmed.

4

First session, guided and supported

Your first session comes with conversation starters and post-session prompts. You’ll never be left wondering what to say next.

5

One hour, every week

Same time, same person, every week. The relationship builds naturally and your impact tracker shows you exactly how it grows.

Safety first

How We Keep You Both Safe.

Safety isn’t a feature — it’s the foundation everything else is built on.

Background check — every mentor

No exceptions, no shortcuts. Fully secure and confidential screening before any session takes place.

AI Chaperone on every call

Real-time monitoring across 12 risk categories. Transparent to you, invisible in the session.

Parents always in the loop

Parents receive session summaries and approve all sessions for children under 13.

Human review on every flag

Every safety escalation is reviewed by a real Eldera team member. No automated-only responses.

Your role

You Don’t Need to Have All the Answers.
Just Show Up.

Here’s what a typical Eldera mentorship looks like week to week.

Weekly 1:1 sessions

One hour, once a week, over Eldera’s secure platform. Guided conversation prompts available. You lead at your own pace.

Session summaries

After each session, you receive a brief summary and prompts for next time — so you can pick right back up where you left off.

Impact tracking

Your Social Health Tracker shows sessions completed, milestones reached, and the cumulative time you’ve invested in a young life.

Community events

Monthly mentor circles, themed discussions, and special events connect you with other Eldera mentors across the country.

Mentor development

Optional workshops on topics like active listening, navigating difficult conversations, and supporting young people through transitions.

Quarterly wisdom report

Every quarter, you receive a report showing the collective impact of the Eldera community — including yours.
