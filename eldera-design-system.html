Usage Notes
The wordmark is Newsreader italic at weight 500, always lowercase. Minimum clear space around the logo equals the cap height of the "e". On dark backgrounds, prefer white over gold for the wordmark — gold is reserved for the tagline or accent text. Gold on clay fails contrast (2.37:1) — use dark brown on clay instead. Never stretch, rotate, or set the wordmark in a different typeface.

Rules
Never use white text on gold backgrounds. Always dark brown on gold.
Gold text on light backgrounds (white, clay) is decorative only — it must always accompany legible text and never carry critical information alone. Use for section labels, eyebrows, and italic accents in headlines where the surrounding text is readable.
On dark backgrounds: white for body text, gold for accents and labels. Both pass AA.
Minimum font size: 0.78rem (caption). Nothing smaller — this is a 60+ audience.
